My Peanut Butter Celery Recipe

Description

A healthy, fun classic.

Ingredients

  • Celery
  • Peanut Butter (Crunchy for fun)
  • Cacao Nibs or Dark Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Snap apart celery from main stalk
  2. Apply peanut butter to groove of celery
  3. Place nibs or chocolate chips into peanut butter
  4. eat and smile
