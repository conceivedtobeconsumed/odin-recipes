My Hummus Recipe

Description

Delicious hummus with kalamata olives and harissa for a spicy kick

Ingredients

  • Garbonzo Beans
  • Tahini (ground sesame seeds)
  • Olive Oil
  • Kalamata Olives
  • Harissa
  • Garlic Clove
  • Lime Juice
  • Fresh Ground Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Grab your blender
  2. Add all of your ingrediants to the blender
  3. Blend until smooth
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste
  5. Pour into a bowl or storage container for later use
