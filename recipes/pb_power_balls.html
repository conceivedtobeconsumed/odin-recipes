Peanut Butter Power Balls

Description

Delicious no grain peanut butter protein balls.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Unsweetened Coconut
  • Flax Seed
  • Protein Powder
  • Allulose (non-digestable sweetener)
  • [Optional] Maple Syrup
  • [Optional] Dark Chocolate (>=60% Recommended)

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a large mixing bowl
  2. Stir until thoroughly mixed and substance can be handled with your hands
  3. Roll into 2.5 cm balls and set on a cookie sheet with parchment paper, keep them spaced apart until dry
  4. Once all peanut butter balls have been rolled place cookie sheet in refridgerator for 1 hour
  5. Once cool you can move to longer-term storage
  6. Recommend storing in freezer and eating directly from freezer (not required)
